About Digital Dose

Established in 2022, Digital Dose was born with a singular mission: to become Addis Ababa's most successful, creative, and pioneering media consulting agency.

Our approach to each client project is fresh and innovative, as we craft customized strategies tailored to your specific requirements. While we have grown since our inception, our core values remain intact.

Vision

Create an active digital landscape in Ethiopia and beyond, revolutionizing brand presentation through all promotional platforms.

Mission
